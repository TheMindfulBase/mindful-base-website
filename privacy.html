Introduction

The Mindful BAse ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, participate in our events, or use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Event Registration: Information provided when registering for workshops, retreats, or events
  • Communication: Messages sent through our contact forms or email
  • Payment Information: Billing details for event fees (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Health Information: Basic health/wellness information relevant to participation in our activities (collected only when necessary for safety purposes)

Information Automatically Collected

  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on site
  • Cookies: We may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience
  • Device Information: Type of device, operating system, unique device identifiers

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services and events
  • Process event registrations and payments
  • Communicate with you about upcoming events and activities
  • Send newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Ensure participant safety during activities
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze website usage to improve user experience

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing
  • Email marketing (e.g., Mailchimp)
  • Website hosting and analytics
  • Event management platforms

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure participant safety
  • Investigate potential violations of our terms

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal limitations)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data in a structured format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we process your information

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure data transmission (SSL encryption)
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments
  • Secure storage of physical and digital records

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

International Data Transfers

As we are based in Austria (EU), we comply with GDPR requirements. If we transfer data outside the EU, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

What We Do

Our mission: We create a space where people can slow down, listen better, and build connections that matter - for themselves, their communities, and the planet.

Our interests: Mental well-being, mindfulness, effective communication, environmental sustainability, and science outreach.

What is mindfulness? Mindfulness is the ability of focusing on the present moment while observing ourselves and the surrounding environment in an open and non-judgmental way.

What we do: We keep things simple but effective - using workshops, group activities, meditation and time in nature to help people feel more connected and included.

What We Offer

Effective and Mindful Communication Workshop

How to connect with ourselves and others
Ever notice how the most important conversations can be the hardest ones? What if you could turn those challenging moments into opportunities for deeper connection? This interactive workshop combines mindfulness practices with practical communication tools to help you listen more deeply, speak more thoughtfully, and navigate difficult conversations with mindfulness and compassion. No prior experience required - all tools are immediately applicable to daily interactions.
Online via Zoom (Interactive Format)

Format: 90-minute sessions, offered in two parts

Language: English

Registration: Google form

Cost: Free

📅 Dates:
Part I: Tuesday, September 16, 2025 | 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM CET
Part II: Tuesday, September 23, 2025 | 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM CET
Part I Features:
  • Brief mindfulness arrival practice and breathing techniques
  • Real-life communication sustainability examples
  • Active listening techniques for professional settings
  • WAIT & AWE communication strategies
  • Practical 10-15 minute activities
Part II Features:
  • Recap and reflection on active listening challenges
  • "Four elements of right speech" adapted for professional settings
  • "You are partly right" technique for constructive feedback
  • Handling difficult conversations in academic environments
  • Practical perspective-taking exercises

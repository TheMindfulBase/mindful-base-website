About Us

Riccardo

Riccardo Trapannone, PhD
LinkedIn
I was born and grew up in Italy. After studying Molecular and Cellular Biology in Rome, I moved to the UK for a few years to carry out my PhD. Finally, I settled in the beautiful city of Vienna to conduct research in the field of stem cells and neurobiology. A few years ago, I got engaged in topics related to communication and sustainability and I'm now working as a freelancer environmental consultant. Mindful communication for me is an essential tool for bringing people together and working on a common goal, such as a sustainable future. In my free time, I enjoy doing yoga and outdoor activities. I'm also an amateur pianist and singer.

Adrián

Adrián Klepe, MSc
LinkedIn
I am from a small village in South Hungary. After studying Neurobiology and Anthropology in Szeged, I worked in biomedical research in Denmark and in Hungary. To carry out my PhD in Neuroscience about the communication in the brain, I moved to Vienna four years ago. While I stepped into the final phase of the doctoral degree, I became passionate about learning more on hatha yoga, pranayama, mindfulness and restorative practices. My vision is to bridge different audiences with the help of communication, neuromindfulness and mental awareness. In my free time, I am fond of sustainable applications in life and spending time in nature.
